About Us

Who are we ?

Homecare Spiritual Fellowship
International (HSF)

We are an interdenominational fellowship of Believers committed to prayer for God’s purpose to be manifest in Families, the Nation and in Evangelism. Since incorporation in 1985, Homecare has grown tremendously to over 1000 members, spread over several branches in Kenya and abroad. The year-round, weekly prayer forums bear testimony to God’s restoration and empowerment. We give God all the glory.
